Humidity Data

This visualization displays a cities' humidty in relation to its latitude. We can see more cities have over 50% humidity! There are many variables to consider that could impact a cities' humidity. Some other variables to consider other than a cities' latitude and longitude could be the season currently affecting the city (e.g. summer vs. winter), proximity to other bodies of water or lack thereof, or other geological structures.
